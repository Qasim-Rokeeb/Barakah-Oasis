About Us

In a world where economic hardships often leave basic needs unmet, Barakah Oasis Foundation is dedicated to supporting our Muslim community, empowering them to live with dignity and hope.

Ramadan after Ramadan, we have been honored to serve those in need. Our goal is to expand our impaact and serve our community in even greater ways, during Ramadan and beyond.

Mission

To ease the hardships faced by fellow Muslims, providing comfort and releif and empowering them

Vision

To inspire a world where Muslims can flourish, unecumbered by hardship, empowered by faith and compassion
